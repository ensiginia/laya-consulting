Our Process

Our Streamlined Process Bringing You From Concept to Creation

1

Auditing and onboarding

We carefully review your existing email data to formulate a plan of attack with accurate KPIs to look for and precise timelines on when we expect to achieve them by.

2

Account set up

Unlike most agencies that focus on setting up flows first and then campaigns, our team moves fast and executes on flows and campaigns in parallel, hence why we are super fast to deliver results.

3

Growth and optimisation

Once we get the account to a solid baseline, we consistently produce fresh content and run split tests on flows to drive long-term performance improvements.

4

Long-term vision

In addition to micro-level metrics, we focus on improving macro-level KPIs like LTV, repeat purchase rate, and churn rate, which sets us apart from other retention partners. This is what true retention marketing is.
